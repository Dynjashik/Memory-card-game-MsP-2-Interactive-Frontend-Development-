MATCH A DOG

How to play


Match a dog is a win-win memory game with 3 difficulty levels:
  • Easy - 8 cards
  • Medium - 12 cards
  • Hard - 16 cards
The aim of the game is to match all pairs of dogs as fast as possible and with minimum number of steps.
Remember, you may flip only 2 cards at a time. If it is a match, they will remain face up. Otherwise you will need to find matching cards around all available ones.
You can restart the game anytime.

About
